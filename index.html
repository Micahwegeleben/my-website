TaylorAnne's Daycare

This is a website containing multiple iterations of a flyer intended to be used to advertise TaylorAnne Wegeleben's business as a daycare teacher. She will be working alongside the local club called Kitsap Tennis and Athletic Center to host an affordable and enjoyable experience for parents that need time at work, away, or to enjoy together. The intention of these flyers is to iterate on design and find which works best in use of advertisement.

Please click through the flyers to see the different layouts that have been made.
